Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

At Finally Fit Spokane (Kairos Nutrition), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when calculating macros, registering for programs, or signing up for subscriptions. This includes:

  • Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Fitness objectives, height, weight, activity levels, and dietary preferences.
  • Transaction details when subscribing via Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to calculate your macro blueprints, customize your meal portions, process payments, and send weekly order notifications (SMS and Email).

3. SMS Messaging & Consent Disclosures (Carrier Compliant)

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages (SMS/MMS) from Finally Fit Spokane for weekly meal swap notifications, order confirmations, and system updates.

Important SMS Privacy Policy:
No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

You can opt-out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

4. Information Security

We implement secure protocols (such as Stripe for transaction processing and Supabase secure databases) to ensure your personal data is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: kairosmealpreps@gmail.com
